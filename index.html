The average yearly cost of owning a standard car in America and using it to get to and from work can rack up to $9,000 USD. A new commuter bike can retail anywhere from $250-$1,500 and with our low maintenance fee that goes straight into our organization to promote our mission, you’re looking at savings of around $7,000! Not to mention you’ll be reducing the amount of chemicals emitted into the air by lowering the amount of drives you take.

Our shop offers over 30 different donated bikes that citizens of Rochester are able to purchase for a discounted price. Get a bike that is regularly $1,000 for as low as $100! Or feel free to contribute to our shop and donate that old bike in your garage that no one uses.

Cars and trucks are responsible for nearly 1/5th of the US greenhouse gas emissions, with a solo driver releasing roughy 1.2 pounds of CO2 per mile. Why hurt the environment when you can help it? And don’t worry about the traffic. Since the summer of 2011, 64 lane miles of street bicycle facilities have been installed and there is an additional 140 miles planned for Rochester, NY!
