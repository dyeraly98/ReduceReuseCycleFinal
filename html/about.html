Our main goal is to promote safe bicycling nationwide, but starting in the Greater Rochester area. We want to help people become healthy and environmentally friendly, while making friends and having fun!

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to facilitating bicycling amongst people of all age and ability levels. Our passion for bicycling is inexpressible. We provide a variety of different services and programs such as bicycling classes, bike repairs, safety equipment, educating people of the benefits of biking and more!
