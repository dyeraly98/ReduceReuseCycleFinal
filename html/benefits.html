Biking helps you become the best version of yourself. A person can reduce their risk of heart disease by almost 50% bicycling for only three hours a week (League of American Bicyclists). Biking is an easy and convenient way to stay in good shape and healthy.

Make an impact on the environment by reducing your carbon footprint. By biking instead of driving, you are able to substantially reduce your personal negative emissions onto the earth.

Biking helps you meet more people in the Greater Rochester community. By attending biking seminars and classes, you are able to make friends while being educated on the positive effects that biking can have. We push for more bike lanes around the city area to provide a safe and convenient way to use your bike to get from Point A to Point B!
